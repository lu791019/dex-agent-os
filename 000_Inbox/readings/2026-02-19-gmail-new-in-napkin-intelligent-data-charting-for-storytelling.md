# New in Napkin: Intelligent data charting for storytelling 🚀

> From: Napkin AI <contact@napkin.ai>
> Date: 2026-02-19

mailer
From spreadsheets to beautiful story-led visuals that actually communicate, not just show numbers
Hi Dex,
For most people, creating data visuals still looks the same: open a spreadsheet, structure your data, pick a chart type, spend too long formatting. And even after all that effort, the result often falls short – charts that show numbers without explaining what they mean or why they matter. We wanted to change that.
Introducing intelligent data charting in Napkin to turn numbers into visual stories
Instead of picking a chart first and forcing your data to fit, you simply upload your data (messy, clean, structured, or unstructured), and Napkin's AI reads it, understands the relationships, and generates multiple chart options – so you pick the one that makes the most sense for your story.
But it's not just about generating charts – it's about enabling data storytelling. Add annotations to highlight key insights, apply your brand themes, and edit any element to refine your message. The result: visuals that don't just display data, but explain what it means and why it matters.
To enable that storytelling, Napkin supports both simple charts like multi-line graphs and grouped bars for everyday comparisons and trends, and complex visuals like Sankey diagrams, Waterfall charts, and Gantt timelines for showing flow, change, and planning – giving you the depth to communicate what matters.
Data visualization was meant to create clarity, not friction. Napkin brings that promise back by letting intelligence handle the mechanics, so you focus on meaning. This is another step toward a future where formatting disappears and your only job is thinking and communicating what matters.
